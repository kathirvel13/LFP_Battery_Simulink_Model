# LiFePO<sub>4</sub> Battery Simulink Model and LabVIEW Battery Monitoring System
A MATLAB/Simulink based Digital Twin and LabVIEW Battery Monitoring Interface of a 4-cell 12.8V 30Ah LiFePO<sub>4</sub> (LFP) battery.

The digital twin reproduces the electrical and thermal behavior of a lithium iron phosphate battery using the Simscape Battery (Table-Based) model. This was created to study battery behavior under different charging and discharging conditions, monitor key battery parameters, and provide a foundation for future battery health prediction and energy storage applications.
